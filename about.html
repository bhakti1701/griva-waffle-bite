Our Story

Griva Waffle Bite was born from a simple passion: creating desserts that bring joy to every bite. Located in the heart of Ghatkopar East, Mumbai, we (Divya Sawant and Prasad Sawant) started our journey with a dream to craft handmade waffles and bowl cakes that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

Our team puts heart into every creation — from the first batter pour to the final chocolate drizzle. We believe that great desserts deserve great ingredients, which is why we source the finest chocolate, from fresh dairy, ensuring every bite is exceptional.

Today, Griva Waffle Bite is proud to be part of the Ghatkopar community. We strive to create a warm, welcoming space for everyone.
